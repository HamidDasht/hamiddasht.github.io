About

I am a M.Sc. student at Sharif University of Technology. Currently, I am a member of DNS laboratory, and my research is focused on the security and privacy of machine learning.

I received my bachelor's degree from the Computer and Electrical Engineering department at Isfahan University of Technology.

Research

I have a broad interest in the intersection of AI and computer security, including application of AI to computer security and AI security. Generally speaking, my B.Sc. thesis was on the application of AI in software security and my M.Sc. thesis is on adversarial machine learning.

Research Interests
